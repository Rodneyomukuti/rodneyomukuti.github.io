About Us

We are a wildlife research group utilizing genomics and genetics to conserve species of conservation concern and the environment in Kenya. Our mission is to bridge the gap between conservation genetics/genomics, conservation biology, and conservation ecology, empowering conservation managers to make informed decisions based on cutting-edge research findings.
